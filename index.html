WHO IS PERTHSANTA?

Perth Tanapon and Santa Pongsapak are phenomenal Thai actors known for their undeniable on-screen chemistry, emotional depth, and captivating performances. Their dynamic presence has inspired a massive global following.

This universe is a dedicated gateway bridging their official projects—from intense drama series to mesmerizing soundtracks—with the brilliant, fan-driven storytelling that keeps their world alive.
