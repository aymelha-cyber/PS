WHO IS PERTHSANTA?

Two distinct paths, one powerful universe. Perth Tanapon and Santa Pongsapak form one of the most dynamic and fiercely talented duos in the Thai entertainment industry.

Individually, they have proven themselves as versatile artists. Together, their magnetic on-screen chemistry and shared passion for storytelling have created an unstoppable force.

PERTH TANAPON

Born: March 20, 2001

Stepping into the spotlight around 2017, Perth quickly rose to international fame. Known for his ability to convey deep, complex emotions, he is a multi-talented artist, gifted musician, singer, and guitarist. He is also an artist under RISER MUSIC.

SANTA PONGSAPAK

Born: November 5, 2003 Actor & Dancer

Santa burst onto the scene capturing hearts with his phenomenal dance skills and highly expressive acting. He brings a vibrant and emotionally rich presence to the screen. He is a member of the T-Pop group Jasper, and is also an artist under RISER MUSIC.

DOMIIA (MIIANG)

Born: January 12, 2024

A little red-haired rocker demon who comes in a cool black suit with black and white wings. As the representative of PerthSanta's fan club, Domiia is always ready to cause trouble, create smiles, and bring happiness to everyone.

THE DUO: PERTHSANTA

As a beloved duo, their partnership is a synergy built on mutual respect and shared creative vision.

PERFECT 10 LINERS

Synopsis: The series follows six university students, three couples, with each couple having a separate arc in the series. The first arc is led by Arm and Arc, the second arc is led by Yotha and Gun, and the third arc by Faifa and Wine. Set in the engineering faculty, the story takes center stage specifically between episodes 9 and 16 for Yotha and Gun, highlighting their university life and protective bond within the "Perfect 10" lineage.

▶Official GMMTV YouTube Channel & Viu

LOVE YOU TEACHER

Synopsis: Pobmek is a teacher who dislikes children but devotes his affection to his partner Solar, who is also a teacher. After a serious accident, Solar suffers neurological damage and begins to behave like a seven-year-old child. Pobmek's life changes dramatically as he must balance being a loving partner and a caregiver.

Community Guidelines

To keep this universe a safe and creative space for everyone, please follow these rules when posting:

  • Respect: Always be kind to other fans. Hate speech, bullying, or aggressive behavior will not be tolerated.
  • Relevance: Ensure your fanfictions, games, and discussions are related to Perth, Santa, or their projects.
  • No NSFW/Inappropriate Content: Keep all shared links and discussions appropriate for a general audience.
  • Credit: Always give credit to original authors or creators if you are discussing or sharing their work.
