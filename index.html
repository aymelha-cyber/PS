WHO IS PERTHSANTA?

Two distinct paths, one powerful universe. Perth Tanapon and Santa Pongsapak form one of the most dynamic and fiercely talented duos in the Thai entertainment industry.

Individually, they have proven themselves as versatile artists. Together, their magnetic on-screen chemistry and shared passion for storytelling have created an unstoppable force under GMMTV.

PERTH TANAPON

Stepping into the spotlight around 2017, Perth quickly rose to international fame with his breakout role in Love by Chance. Known for his ability to convey deep, complex emotions, he is a multi-talented artist. Beyond acting in hit series like Dangerous Romance and Double Savage, Perth is also a gifted musician, singer, and guitarist, bringing a raw, grounded energy to everything he does.

SANTA PONGSAPAK

Santa burst onto the scene capturing hearts with his phenomenal dance skills and highly expressive acting. Gaining massive recognition for his role in My Only 12%, he quickly proved his versatility. Santa brings a vibrant, captivating, and emotionally rich presence to the screen. His natural charisma perfectly balances the intense narratives he takes part in.

THE DUO: PERTHSANTA

When Perth and Santa share the screen, their combined energy creates an unforgettable experience. As a beloved GMMTV duo, their partnership goes beyond acting—it's a synergy built on mutual respect, shared creative vision, and a deep connection with their global fanbase.

Joint Series & Upcoming Projects

Their on-screen partnership officially took the BL world by storm with their dynamic roles in the hit GMMTV series Perfect 10 Liners. The excitement only continues to grow as the universe expands with their highly anticipated upcoming project, Love You Teacher, where they are set to deliver even more intense, thrilling, and character-driven performances.

PERFECT 10 LINERS

Synopsis: The series follows six university students, three couples, with each couple having a separate arc in the series. The first arc is led by Arm (Kasidet Plookphol) and Arc (Jiratchapong Srisang), the second arc is led by Yotha (Tanapon Sukumpantanasan) and Gun (Pongsapak Udompoch), and the third and final arc is led by Faifa (Panachai Sriariyarungruang) and Wine (Jiruntanin Trairattanayon). Set in the engineering faculty, the story takes center stage specifically between episodes 9 and 16 for Yotha and Gun, highlighting their university life and the special, protective bond they form within the "Perfect 10" lineage.

▶ Where to watch: Official GMMTV YouTube Channel & Viu
Image 250x250

LOVE YOU TEACHER

Synopsis: Pobmek (Tanapon Sukumpantanasan) is a teacher who dislikes children but devotes his affection to his partner Solar (Pongsapak Udompoch), who is also a teacher. After a serious accident, Solar suffers neurological damage and begins to behave like a seven-year-old child. Pobmek's life changes dramatically as he must balance being a loving partner and a caregiver, facing the emotional and professional challenges of sustaining their relationship under difficult circumstances.

▶ Where to watch: Coming Soon on GMMTV
